About

Christmas candy based on the 26-year tradition of production. During this time we have become the market leader. Despite the increased interest and higher production volume, we still place great emphasis on handmade production and use of high-quality raw materials. Every year we prepare and innovate selected candy and dessert items so that the resulting blend of tastes is a perfect whole that will make Christmas atmosphere in your homes. All products are made by hand (exquisite vanilla rolls, squeezed paws, etc.) Of high quality raw materials (no baking powders, dyes and various substitutes), we use almonds, hazelnuts, walnuts, pure nougat and rum.
